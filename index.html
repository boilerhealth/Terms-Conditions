Section 12

Boiler Breakdown Cover

Our Boiler Breakdown Cover is designed to provide support and assistance for your boiler and the essential parts contained within the boiler unit inside your home. We aim to help arrange repairs and restore heating and hot water services as quickly as possible in the event of a breakdown.

What Is Covered

Boiler Breakdowns Occurring Within Your Home

We provide support when your boiler stops working or fails to operate correctly while situated inside your property. This includes sudden mechanical failures, ignition faults, and complete loss of function that prevents heating or hot water production.

Parts Located Inside the Boiler

All essential components housed within the boiler casing are included. This covers the heat exchanger, burner, fan, pump, valves, sensors, ignition system, and control board — provided they form part of the sealed boiler unit.

Standard Boiler Components and Internal Mechanisms

We cover the repair or replacement of standard factory-fitted parts and internal mechanisms that are integral to the boiler's core operation. This includes manufacturer-standard components only; aftermarket or non-standard modifications are excluded.

Heating and Hot Water Restoration Assistance

Our priority is to restore your heating and hot water services. We will arrange engineer attendance and necessary repairs to bring your boiler back to working order as quickly as possible, subject to parts availability and appointment scheduling.

Labour and Parts Covered

Where a valid claim is accepted, both the labour cost of the attending engineer and the replacement parts required are covered in accordance with our Terms and Conditions, subject to eligibility, fair usage limits, and any applicable service restrictions.

All parts and components are covered only where stated under our Terms and Conditions and subject to eligibility, fair usage, and service limitations.

Important Exclusions

  • Parts, components, pipework, or systems located outside the boiler unit unless specifically included under another section of your plan
  • External pipework, radiators, cylinders, tanks, or separate heating controls not considered part of the boiler unless stated otherwise
  • Cosmetic damage, pre-existing faults, sludge damage, or issues caused by poor maintenance

Boiler Replacement

If your boiler is assessed as beyond economical repair ("BER"), we may arrange a replacement boiler. Where the boiler is more than 10 years old and considered beyond economical repair, you may be eligible for a replacement boiler contribution scheme where you will only be required to pay £999 towards the installation and replacement cost, subject to property suitability, availability, existing system condition, our engineer or subcontractor assessment, and Terms and Conditions.

The replacement offer may include a standard boiler installation only and additional works may incur further charges if required.

Section 13

Central Heating System Cover

Our Central Heating System Cover provides support and assistance for components connected to your heating system that are located outside the boiler but within your home.

What May Be Covered

Feed and Expansion Tanks

We cover support for traditional open-vented heating systems where feed and expansion tanks are used to maintain system pressure and water levels. This includes tank-related faults, ball valve issues, and overflow problems affecting heating performance.

Temperature Controls

Temperature regulation devices that manage how hot your system runs are covered. This includes thermostatic control units, temperature limiters, and sensors that ensure safe and efficient operation of your central heating.

Pressure Controls

Pressure relief valves, pressure gauges, and automatic filling loops that maintain correct system pressure are included. Faulty pressure controls can cause system shutdowns and are addressed under this cover.

Radiators

Standard panel radiators used for space heating within your home are covered for leaks, valve failures, and connection issues. We arrange repairs to restore heat output and system balance where possible.

Radiator Valves

Both thermostatic radiator valves (TRVs) and manual lockshield valves are covered. This includes stuck valves, leaks from valve tails, and temperature control failures that prevent individual radiator regulation.

Interconnecting Pipework

Visible pipework that connects the boiler to radiators, cylinders, and heating controls is covered for leaks, corrosion, and joint failures. Pipework must be accessible and within the property boundaries.

Hot Water Cylinders

Vented and unvented hot water cylinders that store heated water for domestic use are covered. This includes immersion heater faults, cylinder leaks, and thermostat failures within the cylinder unit.

Timers, Heating Controls, and Programmers

Time clocks, digital programmers, and smart heating controls that schedule your heating are covered for electrical faults, display failures, and programming issues that prevent automated operation.

Pumps

The central heating circulation pump that moves hot water around your system is covered for mechanical seizure, electrical failure, and noise-related faults that affect water circulation throughout the property.

Thermostats

Room thermostats, wired and wireless temperature sensors, and smart thermostat units are covered for calibration drift, communication failures, and complete unit malfunction that affects heating control.

We provide assistance in relation to breakdowns affecting the operation of your central heating system, helping restore heating functionality where possible.

Exclusions

  • Decorative radiators or towel rails
  • Damage caused by sludge, scale, corrosion, or lack of maintenance
  • Pipework buried under floors or within walls where access is restricted
  • Commercial heating systems
  • Underfloor heating systems unless specifically included
Section 14

Plumbing Cover

Our Plumbing Cover provides support and assistance for sudden and unexpected plumbing issues within your home.

What We Support

Hot Water Pipes

Internal pipework carrying heated water from your boiler or cylinder to taps, showers, and appliances is covered for leaks, bursts, and joint failures that affect hot water delivery throughout your property.

Cold Water Pipes

The supply pipework delivering cold mains water to your property is covered for leaks, pressure drops, and connection faults within the building boundaries. External supply pipes are excluded.

Leaking Internal Pipework

Any accessible internal pipework showing signs of leakage — including pinhole leaks, joint seepage, and corrosion-related weeping — is covered for repair or replacement to prevent water damage.

Household Taps

Kitchen, bathroom, and utility room taps are covered for dripping, seized handles, cartridge failures, and base leaks. Both mixer and pillar taps are included, excluding designer or specialist fixtures.

Blocked Toilets

Toilet blockages caused by household waste or minor obstructions are covered for clearance and restoration of normal flushing function. Blockages caused by foreign objects or structural defects may be excluded.

Toilet Flushing Mechanisms

Internal flush valves, syphons, buttons, and levers within the toilet cistern are covered for mechanical failure, incomplete flushing, and continuous running that wastes water and affects usability.

Toilet Cisterns

The toilet cistern tank itself is covered for cracks, inlet valve failures, and seal deterioration that causes leaks between the cistern and bowl or onto the floor.

Internal Plumbing Leaks Within Your Property

Any sudden and unexpected internal water leak originating from covered plumbing components is addressed to minimise damage and restore normal water supply and drainage function.

Our aim is to help reduce inconvenience and arrange assistance for plumbing-related emergencies affecting normal household use.

Exclusions

  • Damage caused by negligence or misuse
  • General wear and tear
  • External water supply pipes
  • Shared drainage systems
  • Bathroom fixtures purely for cosmetic purposes
  • Repairs requiring major structural access unless otherwise agreed
Section 15

Drainage Cover

Our Drainage Cover provides support and assistance for certain drainage and waste pipe issues affecting your home.

What May Be Covered

Internal Waste Pipes

Pipework carrying waste water from sinks, basins, baths, showers, and appliances to the external drainage system is covered for blockages, leaks, and joint failures within the property.

External Waste Pipes Connected to Your Property

External soil pipes, waste stacks, and rainwater downpipes that are physically attached to and serve your property are covered for blockages, cracks, and disconnection from the main drainage run.

Household Drainage Systems

The collection of pipes, gullies, and traps that form your property's drainage network are covered for operational failures that prevent waste water from leaving your home correctly.

Internal Drainage Blockages

Blockages within internal drainage pipework caused by household waste, grease build-up, or minor obstructions are covered for clearance using appropriate drainage equipment and techniques.

Clay and Pot Drainage Systems

Older properties with traditional clay or pot-based drainage materials are covered for fractures, root intrusion at joints, and collapse of these specific pipe types where accessible.

We may arrange assistance for blockages or drainage issues that prevent normal household use.

Exclusions

  • Public sewer systems
  • Drainage issues outside property boundaries
  • Damage caused by roots, subsidence, or structural movement unless specifically included
  • Septic tanks and cesspits
  • Routine maintenance or cleaning
Section 16

Electrical Cover

Our Electrical Cover provides support and assistance for certain electrical faults within your home.

What May Be Covered

Electrical Sockets

Standard domestic power outlets, including single and double sockets, are covered for faults such as no power, loose connections, burn marks, and intermittent supply issues that affect safe everyday use.

Light Switches

Wall-mounted light switches, dimmer switches, and pull cords are covered for mechanical failure, loose terminals, and switching faults that prevent lights from operating correctly.

Internal Lighting

Ceiling roses, pendant fittings, spotlights, and standard internal light fixtures are covered for wiring faults, fitting failures, and supply issues. Bulb replacement is not included.

Ground Floor External Security Lighting

Security and safety lighting mounted at ground floor level on the exterior of your property is covered for sensor failures, wiring faults, and fitting damage that affects operation and home security.

This cover is intended to assist with sudden electrical faults affecting normal household operation and safety.

Exclusions

  • Appliances or consumer electronics
  • Fuse board upgrades
  • Full property rewiring
  • Commercial electrical systems
  • Decorative lighting
  • Electrical faults caused by misuse or unauthorized alterations
